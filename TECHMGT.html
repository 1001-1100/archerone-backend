First Trimester, AY 2020 - 2021

IMPORTANT ADVISORY

There shall be three types of course offerings during Term 3, AY 2019-2020:

A.  Full Online (no in-person classes at all)
B.  Hybrid (with in-person classes in one week)
C.  Predominantly in-person (eight days of in-person classes in two non-consecutive weeks)

THE TYPE IS INDICATED AT THE REMARKS COLUMN OF THE COURSE OFFERINGS.

1.  General Schedule
     1.1.  Only classes that are of Types A and B will be offered during the first enrollment period in June.
     1.2.  Type A (Full Online) classes shall be held on weeks 1 to 9, and weeks 11 & 12.
     1.3.  Type B (Hybrid) will be delivered in online mode from weeks 1 to 9.
             1.3.1.  RVRCOB, CLA and SOE will meet face-to-face on week 11.
             1.3.2.  CCS, GCOE, COS and BAGCED will meet face-to-face on week 12.
     1.4.  Final Exam is on week 14. The mode of conduct of the exam rests with the faculty.

2.  Clustering principle for enrolling in Type B (Hybrid)
     2.1.  Students should only enroll in sections offered by colleges in the same cluster where the student belongs:
     2.2.  Enrollment clustering will be as follows:
             Students of RVRCOB, CLA and SOE should only enroll in C, A, and V sections.
             Students of CCS, GCOE, COS and BAGCED should only enroll in S, E, N and L.
     2.3.  The system will drop classes enrolled by students who fail to comply with this enrollment clustering directive.

3.  Students of Types A and B classes will be on independent online learning on Weeks 10 and 13.

4.  If conditions are favorable by August, a second enrollment shall be held for Term 3 AY 2019-2020. Only courses of Type C shall be      offered during this period. Type C in-person classes shall be held on Weeks 10 and 13, daily from Mondays to Thursdays only      Online sessions for Type C classes may be held on Weeks 9, 11, and 12.
